★ Design Process — Fusion 360

Frame

Rectangular box, wall thickness 2 mm. Outer dimensions chosen to fit the build plate with room for the LED channel on the back.

Lettering

Letters M • A • T • T and the ★ were sketched as pixel-style profiles, then extruded and combined with the front panel.

LED Channel

A recessed channel routed along the bottom interior edge for the USB-powered LED strip. Diffusion layer sits flush over the letters.

Diffusion

A 1 mm translucent panel (white PLA or acrylic) spreads light evenly across the engraved text and star.

Wiring

★ Reflection

What worked

  • Pixel-art letters came out crisp after printing
  • Stardew Valley colours translated well through the PLA
  • LED strip fit snugly in the channel

What I'd change

  • Increase wall thickness to 3 mm for rigidity
  • Add a switch instead of plugging/unplugging USB
  • Try a frosted acrylic sheet for better diffusion

Overall I'm happy with the result — it glows exactly like I imagined.
